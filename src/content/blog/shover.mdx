[Shover](/) is a modern UI library built on top of **shadcn/ui**, focused on helping developers build interfaces that feel just as good as they look.

Rather than shipping another opinionated component library, Shover offers a growing collection of **components**, **motion effects**, **blocks**, and **templates** that are easy to copy, understand, and customize.

---

## Why Shover?

Building a polished interface often means recreating the same patterns across every project.

Shover aims to change that by providing thoughtfully crafted building blocks that are:

- **Production-ready** from day one.
- **Easy to customize** without unnecessary abstractions.
- **Consistent** in design, motion, and developer experience.
- Built for **real projects**, not just demos.

The goal isn't to replace your workflow—it's to make it better.

---

## Built with Developers in Mind

Every component is written with readability and maintainability in mind.

Instead of hiding implementation details behind complex APIs, Shover embraces the philosophy of **owning your code**. Copy what you need, adapt it to your project, and build without limitations.

Whether you're creating a landing page, dashboard, portfolio, or SaaS product, Shover should feel like a natural extension of your stack—not another dependency to fight against.
